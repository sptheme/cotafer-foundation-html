Ready to be a partner with Cotafer Foundation?

  1. Submit an application. We vet each of our partners so we can confidently tell our corporate and individual donors they are giving to verified charities. See the list of documents that you will be asked to submit.
  2. Create a project.Share detail about your work with potential donors, demonstrating how their own donation will make a difference.

We know your time is valuable, so before you begin you application, we have a few questions to see if we are a good match:

Is your organization a registered charity, nonprofit, or NGO that is implementing a program?
Can you provide organizational documents in English? (Google translations are ok!)
Click here for more information
Is your organization fiscally-sponsored by a registered charity, nonprofit, or NGO?
Can you mobilize your network to donate your project through the Cotafer Foundation platform?

Sorry, but Cotafer Foundation can only work with registered organizations that can provide organizational documents in English and who want to use online tools to mobilize their supporters.

Terrific! It looks like we can work together!
